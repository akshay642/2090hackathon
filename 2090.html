About the Event

Welcome to 2090 Innovate-2-Sustain the Sustainability and Innovation Hackathon, a groundbreaking initiative that brings together visionaries, innovators, and change-makers to address pressing global challenges.

The hackathon is conducted by the Department of BBA and BBA in Business Analytics, Faculty of Management and Commerce offers a platform to create and develop solutions for real time problems existing in our country

This event is a collaborative effort between the Government of Karnataka, the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), and the Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ). Our collective aim is to drive sustainable solutions that make a real impact on our planet

Event Schedule

Day 1 - Registration & Opening Ceremony

Join us for the kick-off event and registration at 2:00 PM.

Day 1 - Problem statement Challenges (Part 1)

Engage in exciting Speaker Session 1 starting at 3:30 PM.

Day 1 - Round 1- REVOLUTION

The teams must have a brief solution for the first 2 parameters according to their statement.

Day 1 - Round 2- IMPACT CATALYST

The teams must have a brief solution for the next 2 parameters..

Day 2 - Round 3- SOLUTION SYNTHESIS

A final draft which includes a presentation and a report on the idea and its scope.

Day 2 - Pitching - 1

Faculties will be visiting each team ; 15 teams will be short-listed

Day 2 - RESILIENCE RALLY

The 15 teams will work on the changes and improvisation

Day 2 - Final Pitching

The 15 teams will pitch in their ideas in front of judges

Day 2 - Announcement of Winners(5:00 PM)

The top 10 teams will be announced and first 3 teams will receive a cash award and the best solutions will be incorporated into the respective industries

🚀 Event Guidelines

Participants can Register in a team of 2.

Ethical conduct is expected from all participants throughout the event.

Cheating, plagiarism, or academic dishonesty will result in disqualification.

Participants are advised to be present in appropriate attire.

Any fizzed/ coloured/ alcoholic beverages are strictly not permitted.

Participants should carry their own laptops and chargers..

Participants are not allowed to leave the designated area without informing the volunteers and this is applicable at all times..

Participants have to wear their ID cards and badges at all times..

Breach of rules may lead to disqualification.
